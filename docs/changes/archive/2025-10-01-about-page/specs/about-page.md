# @trace 2025-10-01-about-page

## page-access [ADD]

The system SHALL serve an About page at `/[lang]/about` for each supported locale (`zh-TW`, `en`, `ja`).

The page SHALL be reachable via the primary navigation at the same hierarchy level as Home and Projects.

## content-sections [ADD]

The page SHALL display Introduction, Skills & Technologies, Education, and Certifications sections.

Each certification entry SHALL include: full name, issuing organisation, date obtained, and score or level.

## multilingual-content [ADD]

The page SHALL provide fully translated content in zh-TW, English, and Japanese.

Dates SHALL use locale-aware formatting.

## responsive-layout [ADD]

The page SHALL render correctly at all viewport widths ≥ 320 px with a minimum body font size of 14 px.
